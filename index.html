welcome

Where did the backyard garden go? 50 years ago, everyone had a garden to feed their family. Jardins Gardens aims to make this a reality again. Can you imagine having fresh salads all season long, harvested right outside your own door? That's as local as it gets. And have you tasted a salad you've grown yourself? Delicious! Growing your own food isn't just hip and cool; it's fun for the whole family. If you need a hand getting started, we can help.

How it works

  • Step 1

    You contact us. Tell us you want a garden.
    250-490-3600.

  • Step 2

    We construct it. We put it together, fill it with soil, and give you seeds, seedlings and instructions!

  • Step 3

    You consume it. You plant it, water it and eat it. It's that simple.

To promote food security and environmental stewardship on a local level by helping people to experience all the joys and benefits of growing their own food.
